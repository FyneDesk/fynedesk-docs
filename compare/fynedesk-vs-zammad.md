# FyneDesk vs Zammad

FyneDesk is a free cloud-hosted alternative to Zammad with AI assist and no server management required. Zammad is a capable open-source helpdesk with built-in chat, phone, and social media support, but requires self-hosting expertise. FyneDesk includes AI assist, CSAT surveys, contact management, and asset tracking on its free plan.

## At a Glance

- **Zammad** is open-source and can be self-hosted for free. A cloud-hosted version starts at approximately EUR 7/agent/month. It supports email, chat, phone, social media, and has a modern UI with full-text search.
- **FyneDesk** is cloud-hosted and free. All features (AI, portal, KB, contacts, assets, reporting) are included on the free plan.
- Zammad's strength is multi-channel support (email, chat, phone, Twitter, Facebook, Telegram) out of the box. FyneDesk focuses on email, portal, and widget channels with AI assistance.
- Zammad requires Ruby/Rails expertise for self-hosting. FyneDesk requires no technical skills.

## Pricing

Zammad self-hosted is free (open source). Server hosting costs $10-$100/month depending on scale.

Zammad hosted (cloud) pricing starts at approximately EUR 7/agent/month for the Starter plan and goes up to EUR 24/agent/month for the Professional plan.

FyneDesk pricing:

- **Free:** Full-featured plan at no cost.
- **Pro, Ultra, Enterprise:** Paid plans starting under $10/month. See [fynedesk.io/pricing](https://fynedesk.io/pricing) for current pricing.

## Who Should Choose Zammad

Zammad is the right choice if your team needs:

- Multi-channel support with built-in chat, phone, and social media (Twitter, Facebook, Telegram)
- Powerful full-text search via Elasticsearch across all tickets and conversations
- Self-hosting with full data control and compliance
- LDAP and Active Directory integration for enterprise environments
- An open-source platform with an active community and regular releases
- A modern, responsive interface that works well on mobile browsers

Zammad is one of the most capable open-source helpdesk platforms available. For teams with multi-channel needs and server management expertise, it's an excellent choice.

## FAQ

### Is Zammad really free?
The self-hosted version is free and open source. You pay for your own server hosting and maintenance. The cloud-hosted version starts at approximately EUR 7/agent/month.

### Is FyneDesk a cloud alternative to Zammad?
Yes. FyneDesk provides cloud-hosted ticketing with no server management required. It adds AI assist, asset management, and CSAT surveys, but does not include Zammad's built-in chat, phone, or social media channels.

### Can I switch from Zammad to FyneDesk?
Yes. FyneDesk supports importing contacts and ticket history.

### Does Zammad have AI features?
No. Zammad does not include AI-powered features. FyneDesk includes AI assist on all plans.

### Which supports more languages?
Both support many languages. Zammad supports 30+ languages through community translations. FyneDesk supports 20+ languages. Both cover the most commonly spoken languages.

### Which is better for multi-channel support?
Zammad is stronger for multi-channel support with built-in chat, phone (CTI), and social media (Twitter, Facebook, Telegram). FyneDesk supports email, customer portal, and an embeddable website widget, with social channels available through webhooks.
