# FyneDesk vs Spiceworks

FyneDesk and Spiceworks are both free helpdesk platforms, but FyneDesk is ad-free and works for any department while Spiceworks is ad-supported and built for IT teams. FyneDesk includes AI assist, a customer portal, knowledge base, and contact management. Spiceworks includes network monitoring and IT asset discovery.

## At a Glance

- **Spiceworks** is completely free with unlimited agents and tickets. It is ad-supported, meaning ads appear inside the agent interface and in customer-facing emails. It is specifically built for IT support and includes network monitoring and asset discovery tools.
- **FyneDesk** is free with no ads anywhere in the product. It includes ticketing, email-to-ticket, customer portal, knowledge base, AI assist, contact management, asset management, reporting, and 20+ languages.
- Spiceworks is IT-focused with ITIL-adjacent features. FyneDesk is industry-agnostic and works for any department.
- Spiceworks includes network monitoring and IT asset discovery. FyneDesk includes AI assist and a customer-facing portal.

## Pricing

Both platforms are free. The difference is in the business model:

- **Spiceworks** makes money through advertising. Ads are shown to agents in the dashboard and can appear in email communications. There is no way to remove ads on the free plan.
- **FyneDesk** offers a free plan with no ads. Paid plans are available with additional features. See [fynedesk.io/pricing](https://fynedesk.io/pricing) for details.

## Who Should Choose Spiceworks

Spiceworks is the right choice if your team is:

Spiceworks has been a staple in IT departments for years. If you're an IT team that can tolerate ads, it gives you a lot for free.

## FAQ

### Is Spiceworks really free?
Yes. Spiceworks is completely free with unlimited agents and tickets. It is funded by advertising, which means ads appear in the agent interface and in customer-facing email notifications.

### Does FyneDesk show ads?
No. FyneDesk does not show ads anywhere in the product, not in the agent dashboard, not in customer emails, and not in the customer portal.

### Can Spiceworks be used for non-IT support?
Technically yes, but Spiceworks is designed for IT departments. The terminology, features (network monitoring, device scanning), and community are all IT-focused. FyneDesk is built for any team that handles requests.

### Does Spiceworks have AI features?
No. Spiceworks does not include AI-powered features. FyneDesk includes AI assist on all plans.

### Can I switch from Spiceworks to FyneDesk?
Yes. FyneDesk supports importing contacts and ticket history.

### Which has better reporting?
FyneDesk includes interactive charts (area, bar, pie), SLA performance tracking, agent performance metrics, and filterable dashboards. Spiceworks includes basic ticket reports. For teams that need detailed analytics, FyneDesk offers more.
