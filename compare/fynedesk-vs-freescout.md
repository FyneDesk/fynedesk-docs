# FyneDesk vs FreeScout

FyneDesk is a free cloud-hosted alternative to FreeScout with all features included and no paid modules required. FreeScout is an open-source self-hosted helpdesk modeled after Help Scout, but many features (knowledge base, CSAT, custom fields) require paid module purchases. FyneDesk includes AI assist, a customer portal, knowledge base, SLA tracking, and asset management on its free plan.

## At a Glance

- **FreeScout** is free, open-source, and self-hosted (PHP/Laravel). Its core is a shared mailbox. Advanced features like knowledge base, satisfaction ratings, and Slack integration are available through community and paid modules.
- **FyneDesk** is cloud-hosted and free. All features (AI, portal, KB, contacts, assets, reporting) are included without plugins or modules.
- FreeScout gives you full control of your server and data. FyneDesk gives you zero maintenance.
- FreeScout's interface looks and feels like Help Scout. FyneDesk is a more traditional helpdesk with structured ticket management.

## Pricing

FreeScout's core is free. Many useful features require paid modules from the FreeScout Modules marketplace. Individual modules typically cost $5-$30 for a lifetime license. A full-featured setup might cost $50-$150 in one-time module purchases, plus ongoing server hosting costs ($5-$50/month).

FyneDesk pricing:

- **Free:** Full-featured plan at no cost. No modules or add-ons needed.
- **Pro, Ultra, Enterprise:** Paid plans starting under $10/month. See [fynedesk.io/pricing](https://fynedesk.io/pricing) for current pricing.

## Who Should Choose FreeScout

FreeScout is the right choice if your team needs:

- Full control over data and hosting (compliance, privacy, data sovereignty)

FreeScout has earned a strong reputation as the best open-source Help Scout alternative. For teams comfortable with self-hosting, it's a capable and affordable choice.

## FAQ

### Is FreeScout really free?
The core is free and open source. Many useful features (knowledge base, satisfaction ratings, custom fields) require paid modules. A full-featured setup costs $50-$150 in one-time module purchases plus server hosting.

### Is FyneDesk a cloud alternative to FreeScout?
Yes. FyneDesk provides similar email ticketing and team collaboration features without self-hosting. It adds AI assist, a customer portal, SLA management, reporting, and asset tracking.

### Can I switch from FreeScout to FyneDesk?
Yes. FyneDesk supports importing contact records and ticket history.

### Does FreeScout have AI features?
No. FreeScout does not include AI-powered features. FyneDesk includes AI assist on all plans.

### Do I need technical skills for FreeScout?
Yes. Self-hosting FreeScout requires knowledge of PHP, Laravel, web server configuration, and server security. FyneDesk requires no technical skills.

### Which has a better interface?
FreeScout is modeled after Help Scout with a clean, email-like interface. FyneDesk uses a more traditional helpdesk layout with dashboards, ticket lists, and detail views. It depends on whether your team prefers an inbox-style or ticket-management-style workflow.
