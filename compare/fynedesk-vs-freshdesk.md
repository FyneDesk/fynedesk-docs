# FyneDesk vs Freshdesk

FyneDesk is a free alternative to Freshdesk with no agent limits. Freshdesk's free plan caps at 2 agents with basic email ticketing. FyneDesk's free plan includes AI assist, a customer portal, knowledge base, contact management, asset management, and reporting without per-agent pricing.

## At a Glance

- **Freshdesk** offers a free plan for up to 2 agents with basic email ticketing. Paid plans start at $15/agent/month (Growth) and go up to $79/agent/month (Enterprise). AI features cost extra ($29/agent for Copilot, $100 per 1,000 sessions for AI Agent).
- **FyneDesk** offers a free plan with ticketing, email-to-ticket, knowledge base, customer portal, AI assist, contacts, teams, asset management, reporting, and 20+ languages.
- Freshdesk's free plan is limited to 2 agents and basic email ticketing. FyneDesk's free plan includes AI assist, customer portal, and knowledge base.
- Freshdesk has 1,000+ marketplace integrations. FyneDesk includes webhooks for WhatsApp, Slack, and Zapier.

## Pricing

Freshdesk uses per-agent, per-month pricing (billed annually):

- **Free:** Up to 2 agents. Email and social ticketing, basic knowledge base.
- **Growth:** $15/agent/month. Adds automation, marketplace apps, reporting, CSAT.
- **Pro:** $49/agent/month. Adds SLA management, multilingual KB, custom roles, custom reports.
- **Enterprise:** $79/agent/month. Adds skill-based routing, sandbox, audit logs, IP whitelisting.
- **Freddy AI Copilot:** $29/agent/month add-on (summaries, reply suggestions, translations).
- **Freddy AI Agent:** $100 per 1,000 sessions (autonomous ticket resolution).

A 10-agent team on Pro with AI Copilot: roughly $780/month ($9,360/year).

FyneDesk pricing:

- **Free:** Full-featured plan at no cost. Ticketing, email-to-ticket, customer portal, knowledge base, AI assist, contacts, reporting, and more.
- **Pro, Ultra, Enterprise:** Paid plans starting under $10/month. See [fynedesk.io/pricing](https://fynedesk.io/pricing) for current pricing.

## FAQ

### Does Freshdesk have a free plan?
Yes, Freshdesk offers a free plan for up to 2 agents with email and social ticketing, a basic knowledge base, and ticket trend reports. AI features, SLA management, and multilingual support require paid plans.

### Is FyneDesk free plan better than Freshdesk free plan?
FyneDesk's free plan includes more features: AI assist, customer portal, knowledge base with categories, contact management, asset management, and reporting. Freshdesk's free plan is limited to basic email ticketing for 2 agents.

### Can I switch from Freshdesk to FyneDesk?
Yes. FyneDesk supports importing contacts and ticket history from other platforms.

### Does FyneDesk have social media support like Freshdesk?
FyneDesk does not include native social media channels. It supports email, customer portal, and embeddable widget as channels. Social media integration is available through webhooks.

### How does Freshdesk AI compare to FyneDesk AI?
Freshdesk AI (Freddy) requires paid add-ons: $29/agent/month for Copilot and $100 per 1,000 sessions for the AI Agent. FyneDesk includes AI assist (summarize, suggest, draft, classify, customer-facing AI, AI KB articles) on all plans.

### Is Freshdesk ad-free?
Yes, Freshdesk is ad-free (unlike Spiceworks). FyneDesk is also ad-free.
