# FyneDesk vs Zendesk

FyneDesk is a free alternative to Zendesk for small and mid-size support teams. Zendesk is the industry leader with enterprise-grade features, 1,300+ integrations, and per-agent pricing starting at $19/month. FyneDesk includes AI assist, a customer portal, knowledge base, email-to-ticket, contact management, asset tracking, and reporting on its free plan.

## At a Glance

- **Zendesk** starts at $19/agent/month for basic email ticketing, with most teams paying $55-$115/agent/month for the Suite plans that include omnichannel support. AI and advanced features are add-ons at $50/agent/month each.
- **FyneDesk** offers a free plan with ticketing, email-to-ticket, knowledge base, customer portal, AI assist, contacts, teams, asset management, reporting, and 20+ languages included.
- Zendesk charges per agent per month. A 10-agent team on Suite Professional pays roughly $1,150/month before add-ons. FyneDesk's free plan covers the same core features at no cost.
- Zendesk has a massive marketplace with 1,300+ integrations. FyneDesk includes webhooks for WhatsApp, Slack, and Zapier, with more integrations available on paid plans.

## Pricing

Zendesk's pricing is per agent, per month, billed annually:

- **Support Team:** $19/agent/month (email and social only, no help center)
- **Suite Team:** $55/agent/month (ticketing, messaging, help center, voice)
- **Suite Growth:** $89/agent/month (adds customer portal, SLA)
- **Suite Professional:** $115/agent/month (adds custom reporting, CSAT, skills routing)
- **Suite Enterprise:** $169/agent/month (advanced customization, sandbox)
- **Advanced AI add-on:** $50/agent/month (on top of Suite plan)

A 10-agent team on Suite Professional with Advanced AI: roughly $1,650/month ($19,800/year).

FyneDesk offers four plans:

- **Free:** Full-featured support platform at no cost. Includes ticketing, email-to-ticket, customer portal, knowledge base, AI assist, contacts, reporting, and more.
- **Pro, Ultra, Enterprise:** Paid plans starting under $10/month with additional features like advanced SLA rules, embeddable widget customization, response templates, webhooks, SSO, and more. See [fynedesk.io/pricing](https://fynedesk.io/pricing) for current pricing.

## FAQ

### Does Zendesk have a free plan?
No. Zendesk offers a 14-day free trial, but there is no permanent free tier. The cheapest plan starts at $19/agent/month.

### Is FyneDesk really free?
Yes. FyneDesk's free plan includes ticketing, email-to-ticket, customer portal, knowledge base, AI assist, contact management, reporting, and more. Paid plans are available for teams that need advanced features.

### Can I switch from Zendesk to FyneDesk?
Yes. FyneDesk supports data import for contacts and ticket history, making migration from Zendesk straightforward.

### Does FyneDesk have phone support like Zendesk?
FyneDesk does not include native phone or voice support. If call center functionality is critical, Zendesk's Suite plans include it.

### How does AI compare between FyneDesk and Zendesk?
FyneDesk includes AI assist (summarize, suggest, draft, classify) on all plans. Zendesk's equivalent AI features require the Advanced AI add-on at $50/agent/month on top of a Suite plan.

### Is Zendesk worth the price for a small team?
For teams under 10 agents, Zendesk's per-agent pricing adds up quickly. A 5-agent team on Suite Team pays $275/month for features that FyneDesk includes for free. Zendesk is better suited for larger organizations with complex needs.
