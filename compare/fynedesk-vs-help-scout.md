# FyneDesk vs Help Scout

FyneDesk is a free alternative to Help Scout with no contact volume limits. Help Scout's free plan caps at 50 contacts per month. FyneDesk includes AI assist, SLA tracking, a customer portal, knowledge base, contact management, and asset tracking on its free plan.

## At a Glance

- **Help Scout** recently switched to contact-based pricing. The free plan handles up to 50 contacts/month. Paid plans start at $50/month (Standard) for 100 contacts. AI Answers costs $0.75 per resolution on top of plan pricing.
- **FyneDesk** offers a free plan with ticketing, email-to-ticket, customer portal, knowledge base, AI assist, contact management, teams, reporting, and 20+ languages.
- Help Scout excels at making support feel like regular email, not a ticketing system. FyneDesk is a more traditional helpdesk with structured ticket management, SLAs, and asset tracking.
- Help Scout has 100+ integrations. FyneDesk includes webhooks for Slack, WhatsApp, Zapier, and custom endpoints.

## Pricing

Help Scout uses contact-based pricing (number of unique customers helped per month):

- **Free:** Up to 50 contacts/month, 5 users, 1 inbox, 1 Docs site.
- **Standard:** $50/month for 100 contacts. Multiple inboxes, API access, 100+ integrations, advanced reports, CSAT.
- **Plus:** $75/month for 100 contacts. Custom fields, teams, advanced permissions, Salesforce integration.
- **Pro:** Custom pricing. Dedicated account manager, training, premium onboarding.
- **AI Answers:** $0.75 per resolution (3-month free trial for new accounts).
- **Extra inboxes:** $10/month each. Extra Docs sites: $20/month each.

Note: Help Scout charges based on contacts helped, not agents. This is good for teams with many agents but few customers, and expensive for teams with high customer volume.

FyneDesk pricing:

- **Free:** Full-featured plan at no cost.
- **Pro, Ultra, Enterprise:** Paid plans starting under $10/month. See [fynedesk.io/pricing](https://fynedesk.io/pricing) for current pricing.

## Who Should Choose Help Scout

Help Scout is the right choice if your team needs:

- An email-like support experience (conversations feel like email, not tickets)
- Built-in live chat through the Beacon widget
- Phone and SMS support integrated into the inbox
- A platform popular with SaaS companies and e-commerce teams
- Contact-based pricing that benefits teams with many agents but low customer volume
- A polished, minimal interface with fast onboarding
- Native Salesforce integration

Help Scout has built a loyal following among teams that want support to feel personal, not mechanical. If your team values that philosophy, it's a solid choice.

## Who Should Choose FyneDesk

## FAQ

### Does Help Scout have a free plan?
Yes, Help Scout offers a free plan for up to 50 contacts per month with 5 users, 1 inbox, and 1 Docs site. AI drafts are unlimited, but AI Answers (customer self-service) costs $0.75 per resolution after the 3-month free trial.

### What is contact-based pricing?
Help Scout charges based on how many unique customers your team helps each month, not how many agents you have. This benefits teams with many agents and few customers, but gets expensive if you have high customer volume.

### Can I switch from Help Scout to FyneDesk?
Yes. FyneDesk supports data import for contacts and ticket history.

### Does FyneDesk have live chat like Help Scout?
FyneDesk does not include live chat. It supports email, customer portal, and an embeddable website widget as customer channels.

### Does Help Scout have SLA tracking?
No. Help Scout does not include SLA management or SLA-based reporting. FyneDesk includes SLA tracking on all plans.

### Which is better for a small team just starting out?
Both have free plans. Help Scout's free plan is limited to 50 contacts/month. FyneDesk's free plan has no contact limits and includes more features (AI assist, customer portal, SLA tracking, asset management). For a team with few customers, Help Scout's free plan works. For a team expecting growth, FyneDesk's free plan has more room.
