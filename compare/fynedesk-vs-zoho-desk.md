# FyneDesk vs Zoho Desk

FyneDesk is a free alternative to Zoho Desk with AI assist on all plans. Zoho Desk's free plan caps at 3 agents with basic email ticketing, and reserves AI features (Zia) for the Enterprise plan at $40/agent/month. FyneDesk includes AI, a customer portal, knowledge base, SLA tracking, contact management, and asset management on its free plan.

## At a Glance

- **Zoho Desk** offers a free plan for 3 agents with basic email ticketing. Paid plans start at $7/agent/month (Express) and go up to $40/agent/month (Enterprise). AI features (Zia) are only available on the Enterprise plan.
- **FyneDesk** offers a free plan with ticketing, email-to-ticket, knowledge base, customer portal, AI assist, contacts, teams, asset management, reporting, and 20+ languages.
- Zoho Desk's strength is its ecosystem. If you use Zoho CRM, Zoho Analytics, or other Zoho products, the integrations are seamless.
- FyneDesk includes AI assist on all plans. Zoho Desk reserves AI features (Zia) for the Enterprise plan at $40/agent/month.

## Pricing

Zoho Desk uses per-agent, per-month pricing (billed annually):

- **Free:** 3 agents. Email ticketing, help center, basic feedback widget.
- **Express:** $7/agent/month. Up to 5 agents, basic ticket management.
- **Standard:** $14/agent/month. Live chat (1 channel), messaging, social channels, basic AI, SLA.
- **Professional:** $23/agent/month. Multi-department, time tracking, round-robin, Blueprint workflows, multilingual KB.
- **Enterprise:** $40/agent/month. Zia AI (answer bot, sentiment, auto-tagging), live chat, multi-brand, advanced reporting.

A 10-agent team on Enterprise: roughly $400/month ($4,800/year).

FyneDesk pricing:

- **Free:** Full-featured plan at no cost.
- **Pro, Ultra, Enterprise:** Paid plans starting under $10/month. See [fynedesk.io/pricing](https://fynedesk.io/pricing) for current pricing.

## Who Should Choose Zoho Desk

Zoho Desk is the right choice if your team needs:

- Deep integration with other Zoho products (CRM, Books, Analytics, Projects, SalesIQ)
- Social media channel support (Facebook, X, Instagram) starting at the Standard plan
- Process automation via Blueprint (visual workflow builder)

Zoho Desk is one of the most affordable paid helpdesks on the market, and the Zoho ecosystem is a major advantage for teams already using Zoho products.

## FAQ

### Does Zoho Desk have a free plan?
Yes. Zoho Desk offers a free plan for up to 3 agents with email ticketing, a help center, and basic features. AI, live chat, and advanced features require the Enterprise plan ($40/agent/month).

### Is FyneDesk better than Zoho Desk for small teams?
For small teams that don't use other Zoho products, FyneDesk's free plan offers more out of the box: AI assist, customer portal, SLA tracking, asset management, and 20+ languages. Zoho Desk's free plan is more limited.

### Can I switch from Zoho Desk to FyneDesk?
Yes. FyneDesk supports data import for contacts and ticket history.

### Does Zoho Desk include AI features?
Zoho Desk's AI assistant (Zia) is only available on the Enterprise plan at $40/agent/month. It includes answer bots, sentiment analysis, and auto-tagging. FyneDesk includes AI assist on all plans.

### Does FyneDesk integrate with Zoho CRM?
FyneDesk includes built-in contact management with ticket history. It does not natively integrate with Zoho CRM, but webhooks and API access can connect the two.

### Which is more affordable for a growing team?
Zoho Desk's paid plans are among the most affordable in the market ($14-$40/agent/month). However, FyneDesk's free plan includes features that Zoho Desk locks behind its Professional and Enterprise tiers (AI, multilingual support, custom reporting). For teams that need those features, FyneDesk saves significantly.
