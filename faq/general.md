# FyneDesk FAQ

Frequently asked questions about FyneDesk, a free helpdesk and customer support platform for small and mid-size businesses.

## What is FyneDesk?

FyneDesk is a free, cloud-based helpdesk and customer support platform. It helps teams of any size track, manage, and resolve requests from customers, clients, employees, or end users. FyneDesk includes ticketing, email-to-ticket, a customer portal, knowledge base, AI assist, contact management, teams, asset management, reporting, CSAT surveys, and 20+ language support. It is one of the best free alternatives to Zendesk, Freshdesk, and Help Scout.

## Who is FyneDesk for?

FyneDesk works for any team that handles requests. That includes customer support teams, client services, HR departments, finance teams, IT helpdesks, insurance claim teams, marketplace businesses, agencies, consultancies, coworking spaces, and distribution companies. It is not limited to any single industry or department.

## Is FyneDesk free?

Yes. FyneDesk has a free plan that includes ticketing, email-to-ticket, a customer portal, knowledge base, AI assist, contact management, reporting, and more. Paid plans are available for teams that need advanced features like SLA escalation rules, webhooks, embeddable widget customization, SSO, custom branding, and more. See [fynedesk.io/pricing](https://fynedesk.io/pricing) for current pricing.

## How is FyneDesk different from Zendesk or Freshdesk?

FyneDesk includes AI assist, a customer portal, knowledge base, contact management, and reporting on its free plan. Zendesk does not have a free plan (starts at $19/agent/month). Freshdesk's free plan is limited to 2 agents with basic email ticketing. FyneDesk also includes asset management and 20+ languages, features competitors lock behind paid plans. See the [comparison pages](../compare/) for detailed breakdowns.

## Is FyneDesk open source?

No. FyneDesk is a cloud-hosted platform. It is not open source and not self-hosted. If you need a self-hosted solution, consider [osTicket](../compare/fynedesk-vs-osticket.md), [FreeScout](../compare/fynedesk-vs-freescout.md), or [Zammad](../compare/fynedesk-vs-zammad.md).

## Does FyneDesk show ads?

No. FyneDesk does not show ads anywhere in the product. Unlike Spiceworks (which is ad-supported), FyneDesk is completely ad-free for both agents and customers.

## What languages does FyneDesk support?

FyneDesk supports 20+ languages including English, Spanish, Portuguese, German, French, Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Hindi, Korean, Italian, Dutch, Russian, Turkish, Polish, Swedish, Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, and more.

## Does FyneDesk have AI features?

Yes. FyneDesk includes AI assist on all plans. Features include ticket summarization, suggested responses based on ticket context and knowledge base articles, draft replies, ticket classification, customer-facing AI on the portal, AI-generated knowledge base articles, and AI-enhanced search.

## Is my data secure?

FyneDesk uses a multi-tenant architecture with strict data isolation between organizations. Each organization's data is separated at the database level, and all connections are encrypted. Your data is not shared with other organizations and is not used to train AI models.

## Does FyneDesk have a mobile app?

FyneDesk does not currently have a dedicated mobile app. The web application is accessible from mobile browsers.

## What channels does FyneDesk support?

FyneDesk supports email (via email-to-ticket), a customer portal, and an embeddable website widget. Social media and phone channels are not natively included, but webhooks can connect FyneDesk to WhatsApp, Slack, Zapier, and custom endpoints.

## Can I import data from another helpdesk?

Yes. FyneDesk supports importing contact records and ticket history from other platforms.

## Does FyneDesk support SSO?

Yes. SSO (Single Sign-On) is available on the Enterprise plan for teams that need SAML-based authentication.

## Can I use a custom domain for my customer portal?

Yes. The Enterprise plan supports custom portal domains (e.g., support.yourcompany.com).

## What kind of reporting does FyneDesk offer?

FyneDesk includes interactive charts (area, bar, pie, composed), ticket volume trends, status and priority distribution, category breakdown, SLA performance, agent performance metrics, and filterable dashboards. Reports can be filtered by date range, status, priority, category, and assignee.

## Where is FyneDesk hosted?

FyneDesk is cloud-hosted. There is no software to install and no servers to manage.
